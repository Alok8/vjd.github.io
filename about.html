Objectives & Goals:

  • The party will maintain true devotion and loyalty to the Constitution of India established by law and to the principles of socialism, secularism and democracy and will keep India's sovereignty, unity and integrity intact.

  • The party is committed to the creation of an India that is a strong, prosperous, equality and self-supporting nation whose vision is modern, progressive, from its traditional Indian culture, national heritage culture and values, and Dr. BR Ambedkar, Subhash Chandra Bosh, Mahatma Gandhi Taking inspiration from Chandrasekhar Azad, Asafaqullah Khan, Sardar Bhagat Singh, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel and Veer Abdul Hameed Ji, the party will believe in democracy, secularism and socialism and nationalism. The party believes in a system in which decentralization of economic and political power is definitely done. The party recognizes the right to protest in a peaceful and democratic manner. The main objective of forming a party is that the country should be freed from hunger, corruption, malpractices, misconduct, adultery, administrative and administrative evils and every citizen gets justice.

  • The main ideology of the party will be against the exploitation of the weaker section of the society. They will be included in the mainstream of the country through social and economic change and their political participation will be ensured at various levels. For this, political activities will be given participation in the government. Periodically, various movements and rallies will be organized to awaken the citizens.

  • The main ideology of the party will be against the communal powers and the work of good faith will be done by creating religious brotherhood in the country. The main function of the party will be to educate every class of religion and caste by providing bread cloth and houses.

  • According to the Indian Constitution, every citizen of India will be equal in terms of law. Wherever there will be a policy of discrimination on anyone, the party will fight for it in constitutional ways.

  • The party will struggle to get their rights on the basis of population and on the basis of that, political participation will be ensured. Action will be taken as per the constitution to raise the social, economic, educational and political level of the citizens of weaker sections in the society.

  • No member of any organization having faith based on religion will be a member of the party. The party believes in the principle of giving special opportunities to women. The party will work in the right direction according to the Indian Constitution to establish an equity oriented society.

  • Party members will not indulge in any kind of violence nor promote it. All the members will maintain the peace system of the country and will always support the law.
