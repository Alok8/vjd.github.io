Election Manifesto of Vasundhara Jan Vikas Dal Election for the year 2020-21 Bihar Legislative Assembly

Election: Camera

Honorable,

The entire people of Bihar State vote our election symbol by pressing the button on the camera and make our party Vasundhara Jan Vikas Dal victorious with your valuable votes. The aim of Vasundhara Jan Vikas Dal is not the selfishness of power but to serve the common people with an open mind and to overcome their problems is the ultimate duty of our party.

Electoral victory is the energy and power of our party and the use of that power is dedicated in the name of public development and development of the nation. It is not only my promise but the determination that after winning the election we will try to solve all the problems of the country and the state.

Our efforts are honesty, there will be no bestowal in the maintenance of fair thoughts. Serving the nation and the people in a dedicated manner, removing their problems is the main basis of the pledge of Vasundhara Jan Vikas Dal. We are there for you, with you and our aim is to protect the honor and domination of the entire nation and people. You will give us victory with your precious thoughts, this is my complete belief. With this belief, our party Vasundhara Jan Vikas Dal is dedicated to solving the root problems of this country.

kind regards!

Yours,

Dr. Satyanarayan Singh R. Yadav
National President
Vasundhara Jan Vikas Dal
